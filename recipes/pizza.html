Pizza

Description

Pizza is a traditional Italian dish typically consisting of a flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomato, cheese, and other ingredients, baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Pizza generally consists of a crust covered in sauce, layered with toppings and finally with a layer of cheese on top.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Start with a crust.
  2. Add a sauce.
  3. Add cheese.
  4. Add toppings such as pepperoni.
  5. Bake pizza in a hot oven (450 Fahrenheit or above) till nice and crispy.
  6. Take it out and enjoy!
